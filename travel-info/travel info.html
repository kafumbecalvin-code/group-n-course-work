Travel Information & Planning

Plan Your Perfect Trip

Welcome to the Travel Information page! Here you'll find everything you need to make your visit to Uganda smooth, safe, and unforgettable.

How to Get Here

By Air

Arrive at Entebbe International Airport, with flights from major international cities.

By Road

Bus routes, car rentals, and guided tours connect all major cities and tourist sites.

Visa Information

Tourist visas can be obtained online or on arrival for many nationalities.

Best Travel Seasons

The dry season (June–August & December–February) is ideal for safaris. The wet season (March–May & September–November) offers lush scenery and fewer tourists.

Accommodation Options

Malakai Eco Lodge Kitende lodge close to nature reserves and parks.

Safety Tips & Etiquette

  • Get required vaccinations before traveling.
  • Drink bottled water and use travel insurance.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Dress appropriately and greet locals politely.
